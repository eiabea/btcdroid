Simple application which shows all relevant informations about your miners working for Slush's Pool

General information:

.) Current price of 1 BTC (MtGox)
.) Your confirmed reward
.) Current hashrate of all your miners
.) Average hashrate of last 10 rounds
.) Roundinformation
.) Roundranking
.) Luck

Worker information:
.) Score
.) Last share
.) Hashrate
.) Number of shares
